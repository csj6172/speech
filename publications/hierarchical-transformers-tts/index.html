Hierarchical Context-Aware Transformers
for Non-AutoRegressive Text to Speech

Jae-Sung Bae, Tae-Jun Bak, Young-Sun Joo, Hoon-Young Cho

jaesungbae@ncsoft.com


Abstract
    This paper proposes a hierarchical Transformer structure to increase the modeling performance
of Transformer-based non-autoregressive text-to-speech (TNA-TTS). Considering the fact that the
text encoder and the audio decoder of TNA-TTS deal with different types and lengths of data and
hence the contextual information should be dealt with differently at each layer. In this work, we
limited the attention of each layer with different ranges to provide a hierarchical structure. Addit-
ionally, to improve the pitch modeling accuracy of the decoder, we provided sentence- and word-
level pitch as additional conditions. Both objective and subjective evaluations showed that the
proposed approach (hierarchical context-aware Transformers) improved the naturalness, pitch
prediction and intelligibility of the synthesized speech samples from the baseline TNA-TTS.
the predicted pitch.
